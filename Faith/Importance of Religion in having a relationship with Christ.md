# 1. Main Claim: Religion is necessary for practicing Christian

## 1.2. Definition of Terms

### 1. Christian

> Catholic Definition (Ceremonial definition)
> 
> - CCC 1229
>     
>     > 1229 From the time of the apostles, becoming a Christian has been accomplished by a journey and initiation in several stages. This journey can be covered rapidly or slowly, but certain essential elements will always have to be present: proclamation of the Word, acceptance of the Gospel entailing conversion, profession of faith, Baptism itself, the outpouring of the Holy Spirit, and admission to Eucharistic communion.
>     

> Dictionary Definition (Baseline definition)
> 
> - Oxford Dictionary
>     
>     > a follower or adherent of the religion of Christianity.
>     

### 2. Religion

> Dictionary Definition (Baseline definition)
> 
> - Merriam-Webster Dictionary
>     
>     > **:** an organized system of religious attitudes, beliefs, and practices
>     > 
>     > : commitment or devotion to a god or gods, a system of beliefs, or religious observance **:** the service and worship of a god, of multiple gods, or of the supernatural
>     

> Etymology Latin religiō (reverence, moral obligation, or sacred bond)

: Religion talks about the ultimate relationship, higher than any personal relationship with earthly beings.

: If religion is a form of relationship, then to have a personal relationship with God is to commit on the religion that He established.
