



# 1: Dimensional Analysis

: basically how data is analysed, arranged, and marked.

#### 1] 1-Dimensional
: It goes leftmost to rightmost
e.g. `list`, `tuple`, ...

#### 2] 2-Dimensional
: Can go left to right, and up and down via rows and columns
e.g.  matrices, `list[list]]`, ...
